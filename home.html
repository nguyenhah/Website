About Me

Hello there! My name is Ha and I am from Seattle, WA. I am currently pursuing a Bachelors of Computer Science at the University of British Columbia.

Im 2009, I graduated from the University of Washington, with a degree Bachelors of Arts in Political Science and Sociology. After spending a few years in the workforce, I began looking to learn new skills to further improve myself.

By chance, I stumbled upon some simple coding exercises online and became hooked. The enjoyment and thrill of creating something new after painstaking hard work was incredible. After several months of self-taught programming, I decided to fully commit myself and enrolled into UBC.
